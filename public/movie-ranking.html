The Best Movies According to Amber

My top 3 movies of all-time.

"They Cloned Tyrone"

This movie was very deep & had multiple messages that made you really think beyond the surface level. It was also hilarious because it was starring Jamie Fox. Here's one of his funny lines:

"You know what? That's why I named you Yo-Yo, because you always come right back."

"The Woman King"

This was a great movie because of all of the action & seeing women be the warriors wasn't the typical plot. Here's a quote from the movie:

"Fighting is not magic. It is a skill"

"Waiting to Exhale"

I loved the sisterhood of this movie & the acting & soundtrack was impeccable. The song "Count on Me" by Whitney Houston was one of my favorite songs from the movie. Here's a few of the lyrics:

Count on me through thick & thin
A friendship that will never end
When you are weak I will be strong
Helping you to carry on
